About Maral

Maral holds a CELTA, a Cambridge Certificate in English Language Teaching to Speakers of Other Languages. Maral has been teaching English to international students for over 7 years to students of all ages and backgrounds.

Maral is born and educated in Iran but lived part of her life in Cypres and Turkey. She is fluent in Turkish, English and Persian(Farsi).

Maral obtained her BA in Applied Mathematics and her MA at Industrial Engineering from Ferdowsi University.

Maral's background in Mathematics enables her to simplifies complicated English topics and encourage student participation in solving problems, whether through games or group activities.

Maral has taught English in American Kultur(culture), Mektebim (Bornova).
